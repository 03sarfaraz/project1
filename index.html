Welcome To Photography

Wedding photographer Jashaswi & his veteran photography team have founded their new venture named BABIES.

BABIES diverse newborn baby photo shoot ideas beautifully capture the cuteness, the adroitness in movements and the loving glances that the babies share with their parents. Jashaswi Das Majumder & his team strives hard to capture the perfect moments, and finally presents them as a timeless gift to the parents to cherish forever.

Newborn baby photo shoot in Kolkata has not yet found the precedence it deserves. They aims at bridging this gap by bringing forward the essence of baby portraits. BABIES also cover programs like Rice Ceremonies,Baby Shower & Threading Ceremony of Bramhin Community & Birthday parties.

Apart from narrating the various hues of parent-child relationships, they are also accomplished in Maternity Shoots. Something that Jashaswi & his team enjoys covering the most is the immense joy in the would-be parents’ eyes and the excitement with which they are ready to welcome the new family member. We have designed a number of beautiful concepts to organize the best newborn baby photo shoot with parents.

A passion for photography and perfection enables us to come up with some of the best frames and memories. Moreover the memories made with the clients while shooting and the cordial relationship they maintain with them gives a very spontaneous edge to their stories.

Hence candid is one word that remains a constant in all the pictures that BABIES click. They knit them into a story that you would want to revisit time and again.

The team is being managed by Arijit Lahiri & Suvojeet Chakraborty along with the photo artist Jashaswi Das Majumder.

ABOUT US

Many parents don’t realize that anyone can call themselves a newborn and baby photographer; but untrained photographers can make what should be a relaxing experience, incredibly stressful. It’s not just about the images, it’s about soothing babies and knowing their cues. It is a great job, but newborn and baby photographers really should care enough to invest in training.


With the long term experience that we have gathered and what helped us in finding and understanding other professionals’ works, based on that now we can confidently say about ourselves that we are professional new born and baby photographers , who are well aware about their field and of styles.


As wedding photographers we have covered nearly 500+ weddings in various parts of India & abroad and we're well known among the mass. As a newborn & baby photographer we have completed more than 100 maternity,new born and baby shoots with highest order of professionalism.

Ceremonial Photography & Films: We cover Rice ceremony,Baby shower, Threading Ceremony etc.

Pregnancy or Maternity Shoot: We find pregnancy or maternity to be the most beautiful stage of lives of a mother. Our aim is to shutterbug the celebration of motherhood. We prefer safety and comfort of the to be mother as our foremost priority. The best time to photograph maternity sessions is typically between 33-36 weeks into pregnancy. We have different packages for creative maternity or pregnancy shoots.

Our strength is Innovations, Research, Engineering, Development, fulfill market & customer demand
